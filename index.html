Nanyang Technological University

Hello!
I am currently a third-year PhD Candidate in Natural Language Processing at Nanyang Technological University in Singapore, in NTU-NLP group. I am advised by Dr. Shafiq Joty and Dr. Aixin Sun. Through my SINGA scholarship, I am also attached at ASTAR-I2R HLT Lab, where I am supervised by Dr. Nancy Chen. I am fortunate to be doing an internship at Huawei Noah Ark Lab in Singapore in 2023.

My PhD focus is on abstractive text summarization. Specifically, I am interested in how to build better summarization systems by going beyond the standard MLE with token-lelvel cross-entropy loss and teaching forcing, for instance by learning summarization at the entire summary level. I am also interested in controllable summarization, parameter-efficient summarization, and how to learn summarization in low-resource setups.

I am extremely passionate about machine learning, convinced that we are going through an AI revolution, and have been in this area since 2016. Before PhD, I obtained a MSc in Applied Computing (MScAC) from University of Toronto Department of Computer Science and a MEng from Ecole Centrale Paris (a leading French Engineering School now known as CentraleSupelec). I also worked full-time as Research Scientist at Layer 6 AI, TD Bank’s AI lab in Toronto, and interned at Thales and A-STAR I2R in Singapore.
